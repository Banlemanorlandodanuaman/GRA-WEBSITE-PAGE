❓ Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get my TIN?▼
Visit any GRA Tax Service Centre with a valid Ghana Card or Passport. You can also register online at gra.gov.gh. TIN registration is free.
When are PAYE returns due?▼
PAYE returns are due by the 15th of the following month. Late filing attracts a 10% penalty plus 2% interest per month.
How do I pay my taxes online?▼
Use the "Pay Your Taxes" section of this portal. We accept Mobile Money, Bank Transfer, and GhIPSS Instant Pay.
What is the VAT rate in Ghana?▼
The standard VAT rate is 15%. There is also a 2.5% NHIL (National Health Insurance Levy) and 2.5% GETFund Levy making the total effective rate 19.25% for registered businesses.
How do I request a Tax Clearance Certificate?▼
Go to the Tax Clearance section and click "Request New". You must have filed all outstanding returns and have no unpaid tax liabilities. Processing takes 3–5 business days.
Can I file an appeal against a GRA assessment?▼
Yes. Use the Requests & Appeals section to file a formal objection within 30 days of receiving the assessment notice. Provide supporting documents.
